Cancellation & Refund Policy

This Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which appointments, services, and procedures booked through our Patient Portal may be cancelled.

1. Appointment Cancellation

  • Patients may cancel or reschedule appointments through the Patient Portal or by contacting the hospital directly.
  • Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled appointment time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment time may not be eligible for a refund.

2. Consultation Fees

  • Once a consultation has been marked as completed by the doctor, the consultation fee is non-refundable.
  • If a consultation is cancelled by the hospital or doctor, a full refund will be processed.

3. Diagnostic Tests & Procedures

  • Cancellations for diagnostic tests or procedures must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Late cancellations or no-shows may result in partial or no refund.
  • Refund eligibility may vary depending on the type of test or procedure.

4. Refund Processing

  • Eligible refunds will be processed to the original payment method.
  • Refunds typically take 5–7 business days to reflect in the patient’s account.
  • Any payment gateway or bank charges, if applicable, may be deducted from the refund amount.

5. No-Show Policy

  • If a patient does not attend the appointment without prior cancellation, it will be treated as a no-show.
  • No-shows are not eligible for refunds.

6. Emergency Situations

In case of genuine emergencies, refund requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis at the discretion of hospital management.

7. Policy Updates

The hospital reserves the right to modify this Cancellation Policy at any time. Updated policies will be made available through the Patient Portal.

By booking an appointment through this portal, you agree to the above Cancellation Policy.

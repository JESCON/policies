Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1st September 2025

At Amala Ayurveda, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name, phone number, email address
  • Gender, age, and health-related info (for booking purposes)
  • Appointment details: doctor, time, date, location
  • Payment information (processed via Razorpay/Easebuzz – we do not store card details)
  • Device details (model, OS, etc. for analytics and support)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Book and manage doctor appointments
  • Send appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Process secure payments
  • Improve app experience and customer support

3. Data Protection

We take industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All payment information is securely processed by third-party providers like Razorpay or Easebuzz. We do not store your financial credentials on our servers.

4. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Appointments may be canceled at least 2 hours before the scheduled time via the app.
  • Refunds are processed only if cancellation is done within the allowed window.
  • Refunds will be initiated to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • If a doctor cancels or fails to attend, a full refund or free rescheduling will be offered.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to your personal data, correction, or deletion by contacting us. We will respond to all requests in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., Razorpay, Easebuzz) to facilitate payments and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to read.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
